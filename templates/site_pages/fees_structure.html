Fees Structure

The ultimate aim and vision to start Trinity Theological Seminary is to train the leaders equipping them in His Word to be effective Ministers of Gospel. Our desire is "TTS will provide the best biblical education at the most affordable price."

This is a nonprofit organization which offers significantly lower tuition than most traditional universities and for-profit schools with similar programming. We work with all of our students as close as we can to meet their particular budget.

Undergraduate Level Tuition Master's Level Tuition Doctoral Level Tuition

Keep in mind that these tuition costs are prior to factoring in any grants, discounts or tuition assistance. With available grants, the payment for most students taking 4-5 courses per enrollment period is between $100 and $150.00 a month depending upon degree level.

To keep costs low, Trinity offers its own financing for students through deferred payments on the remaining balance of their tuition and fees not paid at the time of enrollment.

Have questions about tuition or financial aid? Our Enrollment Advisors are here to help.
