The Graduate Certificate in Ministry is a One-year Bible training program. The aim of the graduate certificate is to equip candidates with a non-theology background to prepare themselves for postgraduate study as a means of developing research skills appropriate to theology and divinity. The Graduate Certificate of Ministry requires 16 credit points for graduation. The aim of the course is to guide students in a systematic manner to the acquisition of the body of coherent knowledge that is the classical discipline of theology as a means not only of preparing men and women for the responsibility of communicating Christian knowledge but also for advanced degrees in theology and ministry.

In addition consideration will also be given to other factors such as maturity and motivation, and the likelihood of satisfactory completion of the academic requirements of the course. Students may articulate to the Graduate Diploma of Divinity or Graduate of Divinity or Master of Divinity.

Admission Requirements

The Graduate Diploma provides a two-year program of study for all who has completed 12th Standard with no formal qualifications in theology, Plus…One year of Ministry experience.
