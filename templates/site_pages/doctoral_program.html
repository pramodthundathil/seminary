Doctoral Programs

The Doctor of Ministry (D.Min), Doctorate in Theology (Th.D), Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D), and Doctor of Divinity (DD) are the highest professional degrees for those in parish or related ministries. These programs are designed for educated, experienced practitioners of the Christian faith who desire renewal in themselves and their ministries through advanced training, research, and reflection.

Busy ministry leaders need a tailor-made program grounded in high-impact online education with strong practical application. The International Association for Theological Accreditation (IATA) has authorized TTS to run its Doctoral programs. This program has been designed to fulfill the vision God has placed on your ministry and provide the advanced training and skills needed to reach it.

Our program specializes in deep personal and ministry transformation — equipping leaders to remain effective, spiritually alert, and aligned with God’s purpose for their lives and ministries.

The Doctorate degree is an advanced professional program created for ongoing development in vocational ministry. It enhances professional competence by expanding theological foundations and ministry experience, enabling graduates to strengthen current skills, address weaknesses, and increase overall effectiveness.

This program is open to ministerial leaders worldwide who meet the qualifications for admission and are proficient in online learning.

Admission Requirements

  • Hold a bachelor’s degree from a regionally accredited college or university.
  • Hold an M.Div., M.Th., or any Master’s degree or equivalent from an accredited institution.
  • Have at least five years of active ministry experience.
  • Have earned a grade average of at least B+ (50%+) in their master’s program.
  • Submit two academic reference letters assessing intellectual and academic ability.
  • Submit two ministry reference letters (one from a church official and one from a layperson).
  • Submit two essays:
    • A two-page statement of goals for pursuing doctoral study (including proposed focus).
    • A sample of academic writing (maximum 15 pages).

Academic Information

Most Doctoral programs are designed as three-year programs for Pastors and Christian Leaders. Doctoral students are required to take 10 courses out of the 22 offered, totaling 1,890 class hours plus a Dissertation.
