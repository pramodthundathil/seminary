Scholarship Information

Scholarships are available for eligible students for all courses. You can Email us to our email ID trinitytheologicalseminary@gmail.com with your full bio-data explaining why full or partial scholarships should be made available for you. Do remember to attach all the necessary documents along with the mail, in order for our management to verify. Applying alone does not guarantee any scholarship, as all scholarships are fully based on management discretion, which may not give any reasons whatsoever for its decisions.
