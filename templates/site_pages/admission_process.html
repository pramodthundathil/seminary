Admission Process

Applicants who want to enrol for a course with Trinity Theological Seminary shall submit an application, available in English and Hindi.

  1. Complete online form available on website www.mytts.org
  2. Upload supporting documents for specific course opted.
  3. Pay enrolment fee and course fee for the program opted.
  4. On successful completion of application, Trinity will mail Student Registration Number, login id with password to access account which includes course details, materials and assignments to begin the program with.
  5. There is a minimum duration for all the programs and courses, if a student fails to complete the course in stipulated duration then they will have to re-register for the course opted as a new student.
